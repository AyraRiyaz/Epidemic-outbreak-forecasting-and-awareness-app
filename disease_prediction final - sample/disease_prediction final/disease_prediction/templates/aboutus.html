About Us

Welcome to our Disease Outbreak Forecasting System. We help hospitals track and predict disease outbreaks using real-time data.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide hospitals with a reliable platform to add patient details and disease information, enabling accurate forecasting of disease outbreaks.

How It Works

Hospitals can add patient details, including symptoms and diseases, to our system. We analyze this data to predict potential outbreaks and provide actionable insights.
